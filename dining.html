À La Carte

Indulge in exquisite starters like Baerii and Oscietra Caviar served with Islay Oysters served 'au natural' with your choice of accompaniment. All expertly prepared to delight your palate at The Restaurant's À La Carte dining experience.

Pudding

Conclude your dining experience with delightful desserts such as Mango Kulfi with a refreshing blend of mango flavors and vegan granola. Don't miss our Cheese Selection accompanied by homemade plum and ginger chutney.

Two-Course Lunch

Dare to try our delectable two-course lunch experience at The Witchery for just £29.50. Choose from starters such as the Wild mushroom velouté with roast seasonal mushrooms and poached hen's egg.

Festive Menu

Experience the joy of the festive season with our Festive Menus. Savor starters like Islay Oysters served 'Au Natural' Hand-Dived Orkney Scallops, followed by mains such as Grilled Lemon Sole with Anstruther lobster for a delightful holiday feast.
